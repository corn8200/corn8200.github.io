Inside the zoo — know before you go

⚠️
The internal shuttle is NOT running right now. There's no ride back up the hill. Pace the climb back to the entrance, or turn around before the very bottom. A rented wagon saves little legs.
🐼
Pandas are at the top — Bao Li & Qing Bao, right by the entrance. See them first at 10, no backtracking.
⛰️
The zoo is built on a hill (220 ft up top → 50 ft at the bottom): enter at top, walk DOWN, then back up to leave.
🕙
Open 8am–6pm (buildings 9–6). Your 10–2:30 window has hours of margin.
🚧
Africa Trail (cheetahs) & the Arabian Leopard are closed for renovation in 2026 — don't route to them.
