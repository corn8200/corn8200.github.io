Zero Build · Duos · Dad + 5yo

The Victory Plan

You carry the fights. He does three simple jobs. You win on placement, not kills. You almost never need eliminations — you need to survive the first minute and be the last team hiding while everyone deletes each other.

01

Before you drop

The biggest free wins — most people skip these.

Play this mode Reload (Zero Build). You auto-respawn while your teammate's alive, games run ~15 min, and it still gives real Victory Crowns. The kid dying becomes a non-event — he parachutes right back. Far more forgiving than the 100-player map.
BR
Casual Zero Build Duos, party of two
Unranked lobbies are stuffed with bots — slow, miss a lot. Free kills and breathing room. No "fill."
AVOID
Ranked
Strips the bots out and feeds you sweats — the exact opposite of what you want.
DRILL
Team Rumble = the practice range
Infinite respawns, zero pressure, great for learning buttons. No win, though — practice here, then switch to Reload.
SET
Settings, do once
Aim Assist 100% · Sprint by Default ON · Auto Open Doors ON · Visualize Sound Effects ON · Motion Blur OFF · highest stable FPS.
AIM
Controller starting point
Linear curve · Look ~45% · ADS ~12% · deadzones as low as they go without drift. Wear headphones.

Why "Visualize Sound Effects" matters most: footsteps become on-screen icons — the kid can see "someone's over there" even though he can't read directional audio yet. Makes his "watch our back" job real.

02

The kid's whole job

Don't overload a 5-year-old. This is the entire list.

JOB 1
Loot
Open chests, grab floor loot. He loves the chest sound — and it frees you to watch angles.
JOB 2
Ping
One button. "See a bad guy? PING him and tell me." Second set of eyes.
JOB 3
Heal on your call
"Drink your shields." A topped-up teammate survives the stray shot.
REFLEX
Peek & shoot from cover
Pop out, shoot, pop back. Never stand in the open.
REFLEX
Follow Dad
Stay a few steps from you. That's the whole aiming curriculum.
The one rule, said out loud When a fight starts, he gets behind a rock and stops shooting — Dad takes the gunfight. One reliable shooter beats a good shooter + a flailing one. Also: ignore "harvest mats" entirely — building doesn't exist in Zero Build. Replace it with "always full on shield."
03

How you win the gunfights

Zero Build fundamentals. These never change.

1
Cover is your build
Never more than one sprint-dash from a rock/wall/hill. Bushes & fences don't stop bullets.
2
Peek-shoot-recover
Fire 1–2 shots, then pull fully behind cover to heal/reload. Greed on the peek = death.
3
High ground, behind the crest
Only your head clears. They have to fully expose to hit you.
4
Rotate EARLY, storm at your back
Move before the timer forces you. Everyone's in front of you; nobody flanks from behind.
5
Never cross open fields
Path edge-to-edge, sprint the gap in one dash, or take a car (it's mobile cover).
6
A third party is always coming
A loud fight pulls people in ~10 sec. Don't over-commit. Most ZB deaths are the third party.
7
"Shoot the hurt guy"
Let two squads chunk each other, then push the low survivor with no shields. Free kill.
8
Shotgun opens, AR closes
Blast (hipfire) → swap to AR → beam them during the pump cooldown. The core ZB kill.
9
Heal to 200 before every final circle
Endgames are decided in single pumps. Enter at 200, not 140.
10
Late game: grab an edge early
High-ground pocket, wall at your back — let the middle kill itself.

This season's loadout

Chapter 7 Season 3 "Runners" · verified ~Jul 15, 2026.

  1. Striker Pump S — close-range king. Chaos Reloader is an equal S-tier alternative.
  2. Flex SMG S — best SMG, ideal shotgun follow-up.
  3. Surgical Burst Rifle TOP AR — best AR now. Swap for a Hunting Rifle if you want reach.
  4. Seven Sliders MOBILITY — near-mandatory. The slide is your cover & peek; ADS mid-slide slows time. Slide on water so they don't overheat.
  5. Heals — Big Shield Pots + Minis, plus a med item for HP on the move.
SKIP
Chaos Exploder Rifle
Was strong at launch, got nerfed twice — don't rely on it. The Surgical Burst is the reliable pick.
SPRITE
Grab one early (Striker = mobility, Boss = HP/shield)
But you must bank it at an Extraction Site, and it resets if you're eliminated. Not a free permanent buff.
MYTHIC
Best ZB boss drops
Skeletor → Reacher Extending Shotgun; Voidblade → Voidblade's Burst Rifle. Contest only if you want them; else third-party whoever fights the boss.
SOUND
Season quirk: play by sound
The sound-visualizer got nerfed this season. Suppressed guns (Ranger Pistol) hide your position.

05

When the kid goes down

This is the part that wins games other people throw.

KNOCKED
Crawling, not eliminated
Do NOT panic-revive in the open. Break line of sight or kill/scare the shooter first, then revive under cover. Tell him: "crawl to Daddy AND to a rock."
ELIMINATED
Reboot Van play
Grab his Reboot Card (~90 sec), run to a Reboot Van (note the 2–3 nearest at match start), hold to revive. He returns with a gray pistol and no shield — get him looted & shielded immediately.
DRILL
Teach him the script
"If you die, keep watching — I'm getting you back. Don't close the game." Rage-quitting kills the reboot.
Keep it fun or he won't come back Cheer every chest, ping, reboot, and top-10 — not just wins. Let him drive the car, do the victory emote together, keep sessions 20–30 min, buy the skin he keeps begging for. "Us winning together" is what makes him want to hop on with Dad.
